Community living where you can be you

A safe place to rebuild with dignity.

At House of Shammah, you are not just moving into a house. You are stepping into a supportive community for people recovering from substance use disorder, rebuilding after divorce, grieving the loss of a loved one, overcoming illness, experiencing rent displacement, or simply seeking a new beginning.

Residents enjoy a safe, respectful, drama-free environment where every person is treated with dignity and encouraged to grow.

Healing, restoration, and hope

Your new beginning starts here.

Life can change in an instant. Loss, divorce, illness, financial hardship, addiction, and unexpected challenges can disrupt anyone's path. That is why House of Shammah was created.

Our mission is to walk beside residents as they rebuild their lives through the AAS 5-Level Integration Readiness System, guided by NARR Standards 3.0, nursing best practices, and Rhode Island BHDDH principles.

HOS provides a structured, faith-centered environment, peer support, wellness coaching, and individualized guidance that empowers residents to move from stabilization toward lasting independence.

"House of Shammah is shared community living with respect for individual personal space."

We do not just provide a place to sleep. We provide a place to heal, grow, and prepare for a sustainable future.
